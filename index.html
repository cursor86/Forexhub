Forex Trading 101: Mastering the Fundamentals

Forex, or Foreign Exchange, is the global marketplace where currencies are traded. With a daily volume exceeding $7.5 trillion, it is the most liquid financial market in the world.

Currency Pairs

Currencies are always traded in pairs (e.g., EUR/USD). The first currency is the base, and the second is the quote.

Leverage & Margin

Leverage allows you to control large positions with a small deposit, but it significantly increases both potential profit and risk.

For sustainable success in 2026, beginners should focus on Risk Management—never risking more than 1% of their account on a single trade.
